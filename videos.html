Nut Buster
(If you love absurd comedies please watch!)


A group of friends enlist the help of the mysterious Nut Buster to rid their house of an evil, anthropomorphic, beer-drinking squirrel. Produced in 2015 while I was attending the Chicago Academy for the Arts, this film was made with no script for fun after a real event that occured when a squirrel got trapped in our house and we had to call a professional to remove it.
